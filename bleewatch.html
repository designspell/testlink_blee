Listen

Record any sound like Doorbell, Fire Alarm, Baby's cry into the App.
Whenever that sound occurs in your environment BleeWatch will Buzz.

Each sound will have a unique vibration pattern, unique color and unique icon.

Talk

Text-to-speech at your convenience. Save sentences, phrases, words, names and create a speed-dial list. Press the number and BleeWatch will speak for you. Also, you can chose between the Male and Female Voice!

Dance

Dance is always fun! With Blee you get vibration patterns for any Music you chose. That way even if you can't hear the Music you can feel it! Be an independent dancer with BleeWatch.

Help

Everybody gets nervous in case of Emergency. What could be more reassuring than getting connected to loved ones in such situations. If you are in trouble, just press and hold the Help! button. Blee will share your location with your loved ones.
